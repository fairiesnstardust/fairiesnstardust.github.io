Minecraft Sculpture

For my sculpture, I chose to make a Pipe Plant from the video game Mario. I chose this object because of its cultural significance to the mario community and just video game community in general. Since mario is an older game, it became a gateway to video games for many people. This plant is just one of the many objects in mario that communities across the globe treasure.
